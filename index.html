Our Story

The Jellyfish
Origin.

Jellyfish Watersports was conceived in 2012 as a family-run initiative with one clear purpose: to give everyone access to our waters. In a state blessed with 44 rivers, 600 km of shoreline and vast backwaters, Kerala had immense untapped potential for safe, world-class recreational and professional watersports. Jellyfish was created to bridge that gap.

Structured as a private limited company to operate with agility, Jellyfish set out to address key lifestyle gaps in cities like Calicut: rising sedentary habits, the lack of international-standard outdoor adventure, and the need for non-traditional, non-fishing employment along our waterways.

Fourteen years on, Jellyfish stands as a pioneer—setting safety standards, advising the government, nurturing communities, and inspiring a new generation of water-confident, eco-aware residents. In recognition of our eco-efforts, we were honored with the PROMISE Sustainability Award in 2022.
