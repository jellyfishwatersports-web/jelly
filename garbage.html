Sustainability Initiative

Catch of The Day.

Where fun on the water meets environmental responsibility. Paddle, Collect, Recycle.

Paddle to
Preserve.

Launched in 2013, "Catch of the Day" is our environmental initiative. It transforms a regular kayaking trip into a meaningful act of conservation.

Participants collect floating garbage while paddling. The waste is then sorted, weighed, and sent for responsible recycling through our partner, Greenworms. This initiative was honored with the PROMISE Sustainability Award in 2022.

Marine Cemetery -- "Worlds first marine cemetery" consisting of more than 2000 plastic bottle-designed graves as a conservation-led initiative to revive aquatic biodiversity.

The Process

How It Works

1. Briefing

Receive safety gear and a briefing on identifying collectable river waste.

2. Collect

Paddle through the river and use nets/grabbers to collect floating debris.

3. Weigh & Sort

Return to base. We weigh your catch and sort it for Greenworms to recycle.

4. Get Rewarded

Receive a waste audit report and a discount on your next adventure.

The Incentive

Earn Your Credit.

We believe that protecting nature should be rewarding. For every bag of garbage collected during your session, you earn a 20% Discount Credit redeemable on your next Jellyfish kayak session.
