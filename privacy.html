Privacy Notice

Effective Date: February 2026

Jellyfish Watersports ("We", "Us") is committed to protecting your privacy. As a Data Fiduciary under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDPA), 2023, we ensure your data is handled with transparency and care.

1 Personal Data We Collect

We collect only the data you voluntarily provide via our booking forms and enquiries. This allows us to curate your experience safely.

  • Name & Identity
  • Phone Number
  • Email Address
  • Booking Dates

2 Why We Process It

Your data is processed strictly for the following purposes:

  • To confirm your bookings for houseboats, kayaking, or events.
  • To send safety instructions and itinerary details via WhatsApp/Email.
  • To comply with local tourism safety audits (e.g., Port authorities).

Children's Data

For participants under the age of 18, we strictly require verifiable consent from a parent or lawful guardian. By submitting details for a minor (e.g., for school trips or family kayaking), you affirm you have the authority to provide such consent.

3 Your Rights (DPDPA)

Right to Access

Request a summary of all data we hold about you.

Right to Erase

Ask us to delete your data after your trip ends.

Withdraw Consent

Revoke permission for marketing at any time.
