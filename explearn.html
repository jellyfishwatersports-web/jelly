The Philosophy

What is
Experiential Learning?

It is the process of learning through reflection on doing. At Jellyfish, we take this out of the classroom and into the wild. We believe in the "Weekend Without Web"—a deliberate break from digital distractions to foster real-world connections.

All our programs are designed to push boundaries safely, nurturing leadership, collaboration, adaptability, and environmental awareness through hands-on engagement with nature. Whether for corporate teams or school groups, Jellyfish experiences inspire conscious action, real-world learning, and lasting change—beyond textbooks, screens, and meeting rooms.

International Standards

Curricular & Recognized Frameworks

For international schools and institutions, we facilitate structured experiential learning aligned with globally recognized awards and curriculums.

Duke of Edinburgh & IAYP

We facilitate the Adventurous Journey section for The Duke of Edinburgh’s International Award and The International Award for Young People (IAYP) across the Bronze, Silver, and Gold levels.

IB CAS Program

Aligned with the International Baccalaureate (IB) curriculum. We provide opportunities for Creativity, Activity, and Service (CAS) through river conservation, physical challenges, and skill-building workshops.

Custom Curriculums

Whether non-curricular or curricular, Jellyfish adapts each program to the specific needs of the school or organization, ensuring progression, authenticity, and measurable impact.

CSR Initiative

Catch of the Day:
Paddle to Preserve.

Launched in 2013, a unique sustainability-led program where participants collect garbage from the river while enjoying guided leisure watersports. The collected waste is then sent for recycling, creating a full circular, net-zero learning experience that blends fun, responsibility, and environmental impact.

Award Winning

Honored with the PROMISE Sustainability Award in 2022 (European Union SWITCH Asia Programme).

*Participants receive a waste audit report and a discount on their next visit.
