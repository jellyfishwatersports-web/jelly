The Concept

Comfort, Flavor,
Community.

Cafe Calamari is more than just a place to eat; it's an international-style riverside hub that proudly promotes local ingredients and spices.

On weekdays, enjoy comforting café staples. On weekends, we transform into a lively destination featuring wood-fired pizzas, BBQ grills, fresh seafood, and family brunches.
