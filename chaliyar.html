About the Paddle

Source to Sea
Odyssey.

The Chaliyar River Paddle is not just an event; it's a movement. Starting from the lush forests of Nilambur and ending at the historic port of Beypore, this 3-day journey covers 68 km of Kerala's most scenic waterway.

Beyond the thrill of paddling, we unite for a cause. As a plastic-negative event, we clean as we go, celebrating the river that sustains us. Camping under the stars, local music, and authentic food make this a cultural immersion like no other.
